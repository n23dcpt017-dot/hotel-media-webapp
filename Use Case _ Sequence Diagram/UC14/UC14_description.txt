UC14 — Tự động xuất bản bài viết (Auto Publish)

Actor: System (Scheduler)

Mục tiêu: Publish scheduled posts on time.

Precondition: Posts with status=scheduled and valid publish_at.

Postcondition: Posts get status=published and publish_at <= now.

Luồng chính:

Scheduler cron (every minute) GET posts where status=scheduled AND publish_at <= now.

For each matched post → update status=published and published_at=now.

Optionally push cache invalidation / webhook notifications.

Acceptance: Posts scheduled appear publicly at intended time.

Components: scheduler service.
